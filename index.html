    Bio

    Noriyasu Obushi is a master course student in the Emerging Design and Informatics Course of the Graduate School of Interdisciplinary Information Studies at the University of Tokyo. His research interests include human augmentation, virtual/augmented reality, and wearable computing. He was awarded his BEng from the National Institute of Technology, Ishikawa College in 2018. He is now involved in applying wearable devices for augmenting human perception and motor skills especially in micrometer-scale environments. His works have been exhibited at SIGGRAPH, Laval Virtual, and the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art, Kanazawa. One of his publications was recognized with an award for second best paper at the Augmented Human International Conference in 2019.

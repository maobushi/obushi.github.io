    Temari

    Temari

    Abstract

    To provide cultural night experiences with tourists in Kanazawa City, we have developped and exhibited our interactive digital installation titled “Temari and Shadow” at the open space of the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art, Kanazawa. Our installation utilizes shadows cast by people standing in front of a projector. We chose Kanazawa’s Kaga Temari, a hand-sized colorful toy ball made of thread, as a motif of this work.
